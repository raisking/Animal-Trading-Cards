Lion

The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the big cats in the genus Panthera and a member of the family Felidae.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: Male: 5.6 - 8.2 ft. , Female: 4.6 - 5.7ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 14 years
  • Habitat: Open plains, woodlands, thick bush and semi desert
